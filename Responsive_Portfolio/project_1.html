Project Overview

Project Description:

SumBuddy is a gamified math learning platform designed for students in grades 8 through 12. As students progress through the game, they explore themed planets—each representing a different grade level—guided by our original mascot, Buddy. Each planet is organized into units and lessons, which include tutorial videos, multiple choice and short answer practice problems, and a mastery quiz to reinforce learning.

My Role:

This was a collaborative group project at Oakland University. I led the visual and character design, creating all original planets, road maps, and login screens. I also helped to create lesson plans and recorded all video lectures (96 videos).

My focus was on maintaining a consistent visual style, intuitive navigation, and accessible design to enhance user engagement and educational value.
